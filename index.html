My Thinking Style

Posted on 21 Jun 2014 by Kyle Daugherty

My primary thinking style is Concrete Sequential (CS), but I also have tendencies for Concrete Random (CR). After doing some research, I found that CS learners have the following characteristics:

  • Like applying logic when problem solving
  • Oraganize and process information in a linear, step-by-step manner
  • Learn best when in a structured environment
  • Are inquisitive
  • Prefers applying ideas in pragmatic ways
  • Attentive to detail
  • Task oriented
  • Struggle with working in groups
  • Have difficulty following incomplete or unclear directions
  • Can be challenged by open-ended assignments
